# Wedding Invitation Story Style Guide

Extracted from 31 Chinese wedding e-invitation templates (hunbei.com). Use this as a reference when creating new templates with story content.

---

## 1. Narrative Voice & Tone

**Primary voice**: First-person plural ("我们" / we), speaking directly to the guest as "你" (you)

**Tone spectrum across templates**:
- **Intimate & warm** — feels like a personal letter from the couple (most common)
- **Poetic & literary** — draws on classical imagery, metaphor, nature
- **Playful & casual** — uses "~", emoji-style expressions, direct humor
- **Formal & traditional** — classical 4-character phrases, parental voice (template 30)

**Characteristic softeners**: 嘛, 吧, 啦, 哦, ~ (tilde for gentle trailing tone)

---

## 2. Common Story Structure

Most templates follow a recognizable arc:

1. **Opening hook** — announce the wedding or set a romantic tone
2. **Our story** — how we met, fell in love (相遇 → 相恋 → 相伴)
3. **Love philosophy** — what love means to us
4. **Gratitude** — thanks to the guest for being in our lives
5. **Invitation** — formal/informal ask to attend
6. **Closing sentiment** — a forward-looking pledge or poetic send-off

### Story Phase Labels (commonly used)
| Phase | Labels |
|-------|--------|
| Meeting | 相遇, 初见, 初遇 |
| Dating | 相恋, 相知, 相爱 |
| Commitment | 相守, 相伴, 订婚 |
| Marriage | 结婚, 婚礼, 永远 |

## 3. Signature Phrases (Recurring Across Templates)

These appear in 3+ templates and define the genre:

### Greetings & Openers
- 好久不见，婚礼见 (Long time no see, see you at the wedding)
- 我们结婚啦！ (We're getting married!)
- 许久未见，甚是想念 (Haven't seen you in a while, miss you dearly)
- 我们要结婚啦！

### Love & Commitment
- 始于初见，止于终老 (Began at first sight, ends only at death)
- 双向奔赴 (Mutual pursuit — running toward each other)
- 细水长流 (Gentle stream flows long — enduring love)
- 余生有你，何其欢喜 (Having you for the rest of life, what joy)
- 往后余生，四季与你 (For the rest of our lives, four seasons with you)
- 爱是永不止息 (Love never ceases)
- 慢慢亦漫漫 (Slowly is also endlessly long)
- 三餐四季 (Three meals and four seasons — everyday life together)
- 以岁月为证，以白首为期 (With time as witness, until hair turns white)
- 天光乍破到暮雪白头 (From dawn breaking to snowy-haired dusk)

### Destiny & Fate
- 兜兜转转 (Going round and round — fate's winding path)
- 人生其实只要两次幸运就好，一次遇见你，一次是走到底 (Life needs only two strokes of luck: meeting you, and going all the way)
- 遇见你是故事的开始，走到底是人间的欢喜 (Meeting you starts the story; going to the end is earthly joy)

### Gratitude
- 千言万语最终都汇成一句"谢谢你" (A thousand words all condense to "thank you")
- 谢谢你出现在我的生命里 (Thank you for appearing in my life)

## 5. Poetic Devices & Techniques

### Parallel Structure (对仗)
- "在快乐的日子里共同举杯，在难过的日子里伸出怀抱"
- "热爱彼此，包容彼此，信任彼此"
- "长到四季穿过蒹葭，长到青丝抽离成白发"
- "爱是，旷野低语化作萤火...爱是，星河掠影自成诗歌"
- "爱无边，无暇，无缺，无常，爱无为，无救，无敌，无憾"

### Metaphor Patterns
- **Life as journey**: 旅程, 航向, 旅途, 启程, 奔赴
- **Love as light**: 天光, 星光, 晨光, 光点, 闪着光
- **Love as seasons**: 四季春秋, 三餐四季, 春夏秋冬
- **Love as nature**: 山海, 旷野, 花开, 微风, 星河
- **Love as story/book**: 序章, 翻页, 篇章, 故事, 落笔, 注脚, 韵脚

### Classical 4-Character Phrases (四字成语/词组)
永结同心, 百年好合, 天作之合, 心心相印, 比翼双飞, 鸾凤和鸣, 佳偶天成, 始于初见, 止于终老, 义无反顾, 披荆斩棘, 双向奔赴, 细水长流, 怦然心动, 如约而至

### Literary References Used
- 泰戈尔《吉檀迦利》(Tagore, Gitanjali)
- 王小波《爱你就像爱生命》(Wang Xiaobo, "Love You Like Loving Life")
- "浮世三千，吾爱有三，日月与卿" (Classical Chinese)

## 6. Invitation Language Patterns

### Casual / Modern
- "诚邀你赴约，和我们一起分享这份简单又坚定的幸福"
- "请准备好你的好心情和好胃口，来奔赴这场聚会吧~"
- "如果你有时间，开开心心的来赴约吧~"
- "主打快乐吃席，好久不见，我们婚礼见~"

### Warm / Heartfelt
- "诚邀生命中重要的你，来见证这场双向奔赴的圆满"
- "收到这封邀请函的你们，都是我们人生中最重要的部分"
- "人的一生有三万多天，很开心这一天，你专为我们而来"

### Formal / Traditional (especially from parents)
- "携同我们的父母，邀请您出席我们的婚礼仪式"
- "协同我们的父母，真诚地邀请您出席我们的新婚喜宴"
- "敬备喜酌，恭候光临" (parent voice)
- "吾家有喜，爱子新婚，良辰已定" (classical parent voice)

### For guests who can't attend
- "如果您身在不同城市，或是繁忙的工作脱不开身无法到达婚礼现场，没有关系，我们已收到您的祝福，祝您万事胜意"

## 7. Section-Specific Content Patterns

### Character/Partner Descriptions
- Describe personality traits warmly: 温和, 温柔, 细腻, 有责任感, 天真, 稳重
- Often written by the other partner about their SO
- Mix of admiration + gentle teasing
- Example: "他是一个很温和的人...宠爱我，却绝不惯着我，偶尔也会像个孩子一样"

### Love Story Timeline Captions
- 相遇："总有些惊奇的际遇"
- 相爱："和你在一起就是最好的日子"
- 订婚："人生小满胜万全"
- 结婚："特别鸣谢你制造更快乐的我"

### Ceremony/Vow Reflections
- "这场仪式，不只是庆祝此刻的欢愉，更是为今后的岁月埋下伏笔"
- "约定在疲惫、衰老甚至分歧的时刻，永不遗忘此刻心中纯粹的悸动与珍惜"
- "婚礼是关于约定最盛大的仪式"

### Practical Tips (婚礼Tips) — adds personality
- "由于新郎新娘减肥失败，如果大家当天拍了照片，麻烦P的美一点~"
- "拍照发朋友圈不准只修自己哦~"
- "小Tips：请和我确定到场人数，方便安排座位哦~"

## 9. Emotional Register Scale

From most casual to most formal, for choosing the right tone:

1. **Playful** (嘿嘿, ~, 吧): Templates 13, 21, 28
2. **Warm-casual** (亲爱的你): Templates 06, 19, 23
3. **Heartfelt-personal** (我们的故事): Templates 01, 09, 10, 11, 17, 25
4. **Poetic-literary** (metaphor-heavy): Templates 03, 12, 16, 18, 22, 24, 27, 31
5. **Formal-traditional** (敬邀, 恭候): Template 30 (parents)

## 10. Key Takeaways for New Template Creation

1. **Always lead with "我们"** — the couple voice is the default narrator
2. **Address the guest as "你"** — create intimacy, not formality
3. **Mix classical 4-char phrases with modern casual language** — this blend is the genre's signature
4. **Use nature/season metaphors freely** — they're expected, not cliche in this context
5. **Include at least one "everyday life" moment** — 做饭, 散步, 看电影 grounds the romance
6. **End sections with poetic one-liners** — these serve as emotional punctuation
7. **Humor in practical tips** — the only place casual/funny tone is expected
8. **"好久不见，婚礼见"** is nearly universal — strongly consider including it
9. **Timeline milestones** work well: 相遇 → 相恋 → 订婚 → 结婚 with short captions
10. **Bilingual section labels** (Chinese + English) are common for modern templates: 婚礼时间/TIME, 婚礼地址/ADDRESS, 宾客回执/RSVP
